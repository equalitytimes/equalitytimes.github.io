About Equality Times

Mission, values, and founder

What Equality Times is

Equality Times is a public legal education and rights-awareness platform. It explains constitutional values, legal developments, and social justice issues in an accessible format for students, citizens, and communities.

The platform aims to connect law with lived reality — especially where questions of caste, dignity, exclusion, discrimination, rights, technology, and social justice are involved.

Mission

To make legal knowledge accessible, useful, and socially meaningful through videos, explainers, and research.

Values

Equality, justice, dignity, constitutional morality, and human rights are the core values of this platform.

Approach

Evidence-led, public-interest oriented, and focused on clear communication rather than jargon.
